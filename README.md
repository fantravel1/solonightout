## 3) Signature IP: SoloScore™ (1–100)

Every city and venue receives a SoloScore™.

SoloScore is not a vibe.
It is a structured evaluation of solo comfort.

### SoloScore™ factors (canonical list)

- **Staff Respect** (how solo guests are treated)
- **Seating Layout** (bar seating, counter seating, awkward table zones)
- **Lighting** (warm, safe, non-exposing)
- **Noise Level** (conversation possible vs chaos)
- **Safety Reality** (entry, exit, neighborhood)
- **Ease of Entry/Exit** (no pressure, no bottlenecks)
- **Solo Normalization** (are solo people common here?)
- **“You Won’t Feel Weird” Factor**

### Philosophy
- A great solo place makes you feel normal immediately.
- Solo comfort is about flow, not hype.
- Respect matters more than trendiness.
